Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about iTech University

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What courses does iTech University offer?
iTech University offers courses in Computer Science, AI, Web Development, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and more.
2. How can I apply for admission?
You can apply online through our Admission Portal by filling out the application form and submitting the required documents.
3. What are the tuition fees for international students?
Tuition fees vary depending on the course of study. Visit the Fees page for detailed information.
4. Does iTech University offer scholarships?
Yes, iTech University offers scholarships based on academic merit and financial need.
5. How do I access my course materials?
Once enrolled, you can access course materials through the iTech University Learning Management System (LMS).
6. Are there internship opportunities available?
Yes, we provide internship opportunities with partner companies and organizations as part of our programs.
7. What are the application deadlines?
Application deadlines vary for different programs. Check the Admissions page for upcoming deadlines.
8. Does iTech University offer online courses?
Yes, we offer a variety of online courses and degree programs to accommodate remote learning.
9. How can I contact student support?
You can contact student support via the support portal, phone, or email. Visit the Contact Us page for more information.
10. What career services does iTech University provide?
We offer career counseling, resume-building workshops, and job placement assistance to help students and graduates succeed.
11. Is there on-campus housing available?
Yes, we offer on-campus housing for students. Visit the Campus Life section for more details.
12. How can I transfer credits from another institution?
You can transfer credits by submitting your transcript to the Admissions office for evaluation.
13. Does iTech University offer part-time study options?
Yes, we offer part-time study options for many of our degree programs to accommodate working professionals.
14. Are there student clubs and organizations at iTech University?
Yes, we have a wide range of student clubs and organizations that cater to various interests and academic fields.
15. What are the IT requirements for online students?
Online students need a computer with reliable internet access and the necessary software required for their course.
16. Does iTech University offer dual degree programs?
Yes, we offer dual degree programs that allow students to earn two degrees simultaneously.
17. How can I access the university's library resources?
All students have access to the digital library and physical resources available on campus.
18. What student health services are available?
We offer comprehensive health services, including medical consultations, counseling, and wellness programs.
19. What language is the instruction delivered in?
All programs are taught in English to ensure accessibility for international students.
20. What is the refund policy?
Refunds are available within the first two weeks of the semester. Check the Tuition and Fees page for detailed policies.

iTech University is dedicated to excellence in teaching, learning, and research, and to developing leaders in numerous disciplines who make a difference globally.
