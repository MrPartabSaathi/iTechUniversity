Undergraduate Programs

iTech University launched its Bachelor of Science (BS) programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, Software Engineering, Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science to provide students with cutting-edge knowledge and technical expertise. These programs were developed to meet international academic standards, ensuring that students gain advanced skills in the latest technologies and methodologies. iTech University continually updates its curricula to align with global benchmarks, equipping graduates to excel in the fast-evolving fields of IT, Computer Science, and Data Science. These master’s programs are designed to foster innovation, critical thinking, and practical application, preparing students for leadership roles in the tech industry.

BS Computer Science
The four-year BS Computer Science program at iTech University equips students with advanced skills and knowledge, preparing them for leadership roles in the modern tech industry.
BS Information Technology
The four-year BS Information Technology program at iTech University equips students with advanced IT skills and knowledge, preparing them for leadership roles in the tech industry.
BS Data Science
The four-year BS Data Science program at iTech University equips students with advanced data analysis and machine learning skills, preparing them for leadership roles in the tech industry.
BS Cyber Security
The four-year BS Cyber Security program at iTech University equips students with advanced skills in network security, ethical hacking, and risk management, preparing them for leadership roles in the cybersecurity industry.
BS Software Engineering
The four-year BS Software Engineering program at iTech University equips students with advanced skills in software development, programming, and system design, preparing them for leadership roles in the software engineering industry.
BS Artificial Intelligence
The four-year BS Artificial Intelligence program at iTech University equips students with advanced skills in machine learning, neural networks, and AI development, preparing them for leadership roles in the Artificial Intelligence industry.

iTech University is dedicated to excellence in teaching, learning, and research, and to developing leaders in numerous disciplines who make a difference globally.
